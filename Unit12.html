Some of the biggest concerns I feel are present is security of data. Especially with algorithms, data can be hacked, training data manipulated, breached and sensitive information taken. It is an aspect machine learning practitioners seldomly focus on, but a huge concern with methods such as IoT relying on ethernet, and the internet in many cases. With IoT most data is collected remotely, sometimes geographically remotely, and stream to cloud servers. This data can be intercepted and variables changed which can cause massive problems with the predictive ability of algorithms.
