22 March 2022 ~ 28 March 2022

Unit 4 learnings

Hi colleagues

Chapter 3 of (Bell, 2020) Introduced concepts used in statistics. We require an understanding of much of the data we employ in machine learning and an understanding of the statistical components will prove helpful in providing background to the datasets inputted into AI systems. Typical considerations are Min and Max values, Mean, Mode, Range, and quartiles of the dataset have been discussed.

Seminar 4 was also done in this week, Introducing and re-inforcing students knowledge on Unsupervised, Re-inforcement, Supervised, and -supervised learning. A case study on diease detection on leaves was discussed. Some algorithm types for this tasks were interrogated, such as Naive Bayes, Ramdom Forests, and Support Vector Machines. We learned that no one model is best for every problem and data set, and much work in testing needs to first be done to find the best tool for the task at hand.
